## English Translation

... Tudḫaliya also made his case as follows: I have sinned against (you,) the sun goddess of Arinna, my lady.I offended (you) the sun goddess of Arinna, my mistress.So I took away feasts and circumcised your (other) feasts.YOU, Sun Goddess of Arinna, my lady, were somehow angry because of the festival, (now) take care of yourself again, Sun Goddess of Arinna, my lady!I want to defeat the enemy!If YOU, Sun Goddess of Arinna, my lady, come to my side, I will defeat the enemy.I will confess my iniquities before YOU.I will never curtail (your) feasts again.I will no longer mix up spring festivals and autumn festivals: I will only celebrate spring festivals in spring, but I will only celebrate autumn festivals in autumn.Never leave the festivals inside the temple (unobserved)!
