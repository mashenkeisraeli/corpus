## Translation

Whatever enemy countries are in conflict (and) rebellious,
 who are not reverent towards YOU, the Telipinu, and the gods of Ḫatti,
 who desire to burn down YOUR temples

## Original translation

Welche Feindesländer auch immer im Streit (und) aufrührerisch (sind),
welche DIR, dem Telipinu, und den Göttern von Ḫatti (gegenüber) nicht ehrerbietig (sind),
welche aber begehren, EURE Tempel niederzubrennen,

## English translation

Whatever enemy countries in the dispute (and), are (),
which you, the telipinu, and the gods of ḫatti (opposite) not honored (are),
But which desire to burn down your temples,
