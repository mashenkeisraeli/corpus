## Translation

Whatever enemy countries are in conflict (and) rebellious, who are not reverent towards YOU, the Telipinu, and the gods of Ḫatti, who desire to burn down YOUR temples

## Original translation

Welche Feindesländer auch immer im Streit (und) aufrührerisch (sind), welche DIR, dem Telipinu, und den Göttern von Ḫatti (gegenüber) nicht ehrerbietig (sind), welche aber begehren, EURE Tempel niederzubrennen

## English translation

Whichever enemy countries are in conflict (and) rebellion, who are not respectful to YOU, Telipinu, and the gods of Ḫatti, which desire to burn down YOUR temples.
