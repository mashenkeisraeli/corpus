## English translation

And so it continues:
“You are perhaps angry
or someone has evoked, invoked (or) pleaded,
(e) that (the man) Evil (e) hostile called you in a secret place?
Here now we will continue to evoke you, invoke you and beg you with benevolence for your divine Providence together with the solar divinity
And we will continue to offer you a pleasant pure ritual.
Abandon that evil man
(e) return to the house of this lord of the ritual.
Turn (e), place yourself benevolently towards him
grant him life, health, physical vigor, long years, the favor of the gods, the clemency of the gods (e) the splendor of the soul;
[To him (?)] Granting sons, daughters, grandchildren and pronights;
[A L] UI [grant (?)] Wellness (?), Obedience [. . . ]
