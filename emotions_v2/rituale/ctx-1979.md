## Translation

Then [to the gods] I speak in the following way:
 “Have mercy, oh gods!
 Behold, of the king, of the queen, and of the children in Ḫattuša [their evil], their impurity, the fearful tongues of iron, [the bloodstained garments?] of the Ḫantašepa deities I have taken.
 You, oh gods, [protect] them!”
