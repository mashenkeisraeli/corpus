## Translation

The pieces of land are assigned to the [gods]:
 the sun goddess sat in Arinna,
 Ḫalmašuitta and in Ḫarpisha likewise,
 Ḫatepinu in Maliluha likewise,
 the protective deity in Karaḫna likewise,
 the [ter]rible Telipinu in Tawiniya likewise,
 Ḫuzziya in Ḫakmish likewise;
 But there was no place left for Hannahanna,
 (so) man remained for them (as a) place.
 namma dU-aš ḫatuga tetḫiškit "also the weather god thundered terribly"

## Original translation

Die Landstücke weist man den [Göt]tern zu:
die Sonnengöttin setzte sich in Arinna,
Ḫalmašuitta aber in Ḫarpiša desgleichen,
Ḫatepinu in Maliluḫa desgleichen,
die Schutzgottheit in Karaḫna desgleichen,
der [fur]chtbare Telipinu in Tawiniya desgleichen,
Ḫuzziya in Ḫakmiš desgleichen;
(für) Ḫannaḫanna aber blieb kein Ort übrig,
(so) blieb für sie der Mensch (als) Ort übrig.

## English translation

The country pieces are shown to the [Göt] tern:
The sun goddess sat in Arinna,
ḪAlmašuitta but also in ḫarpiša
Ḫatepinu in Maliluḫa as well,
The protective deity in Karaḫna as well as
the [for] fellable telipinu in Tawiniya as well,
Ḫuzziya in ḫakmiš the same;
(for) ḫannaḫanna but there was no place left,
(so) remained for them (as) place.
