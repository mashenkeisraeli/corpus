## Original translation

„[Alauwai]ma! Soeben [habe ich] dir Silber, Gold, Blaustein [gegeben]!
Geh du nun
und sprich fortan vor den Göttern Gutes für mich!“

## English translation

"[Alauwai] Ma! Just [I have] gave you silver, gold, blown!
Do you go now
And from then on, say good for me in front of the gods! "
