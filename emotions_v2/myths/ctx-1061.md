## Translation

The weather god sat on the earth.
 The tears flow from him like channels.
 The weather god, with his eyes weeping, speaks the word:
 "Who will be able to bear to see that quarrel?
 Who else will fight?
 Who will be able to bear to see those horrors?"
 Ishtar replies to the weather god:
 "My brother, he does not even know a little sense,
 but courage is given to him tenfold.
