## Original translation

[Eben]so sollst du auch der Ḫannaḫanna Wu[t, Zo]rn, [Frevel, Ärg]e[r] heraus ziehen!
[Auch] Ḫannaḫa[nna ging].
[Dunst hielt] das [F]enster.
Rauch hiel[t] das Haus.
[(Im) Herd] erstickten [die Holzscheite].
Auf dem Altarpostament [erstickten die Götter].

## English translation

In the same way you should also take out anger, wrath, iniquity, anger from the Ḫannaḫanna!Ḫannaḫanna also went.Haze held the window.Smoke filled the house.The logs were suffocating in the hearth.The gods suffocated on the altar pedestal.
