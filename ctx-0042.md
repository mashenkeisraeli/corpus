## Original Translation

Welchem Mensche[n aber] die Götter zürnen,
und (wen) sie vernachlässige[n],
[den] behandelst DU, Sonnengott, wieder gütig.

## English Translation

But whoever the gods are angry with and (if) they neglect, you, Sun God, treat them kindly again.
