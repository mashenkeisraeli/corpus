## Emotional domains in Hittite texts

* ANGER (including Pride)
* FEAR (including Surprise, and Respect)
* DISTRESS (including Sadness, Guilt, Despair, and Dissatisfaction)
* ENMITY (including Envy, Disgust, and Shame)
* COMFORT (including Mercy as a part of Compassion, and Contentment)
* JOY
* LOVE (including Desire)

## Emotional domains compared to Akkadian

The list of the emotional macrodomains supported by AHEC in the [Guidelines](https://github.com/mashenkeisraeli/hittite_emotions/blob/main/Guidelines.pdf) differs from the list of Emotional domains in the Hittite texts (above), so that the domains **Desire,** **Pride, Surprise, Despair, Disgust, Sadness, Guilt, Shame, Dissatisfaction, Envy, Compassion,** and **Contentment** in Hittite material are combined into domains of **Anger, Distress, Enmity,** and **Comfort**.&#x20;

**Mercy** is considered a subterm under **Compassion; Respect** a subterm under **Fear.**

**Desire** is merged with **Love; Pride** with **Anger; Surprise** with **Fear; Despair, Sadness, Dissatisfaction,** and **Guilt** with **Distress; Shame, Envy,** and **Disgust** with **Enmity;  Compassion and Contentment** are joined in **Comfort**.

## Structure of the dataset

The dataset contains:

* lemma
* wordform
* emotional domain
* publication and line information
* context and translation fields
