## Translation

They sent (him this):
 they sent him grain,
 they have sent him the disease,
 they have sent him the disease of the eyes,
 they have sent him the disease of the feet,
 they have sent him the disease of his hands,
 they have sent him the disease of the head,
 (so that) the heat (from) him has disappeared
 (and) it screams.
 n[u kui]t=wa i[yaw]anieškiši "Why do you cry?" (asks the sea)

## Original translation

Sie haben (ihm dies) geschickt:
sie haben ihm Getreide geschickt,
sie haben ihm die Krankheit geschickt,
sie haben ihm die Krankheit der Augen geschickt,
sie haben ihm die Kra[nkhe]it der Füße geschickt,
sie haben ihm die Krankheit der Hände geschickt,
sie haben ihm die Krankheit des Kopfes geschickt,
(so dass) die Wärme (von) ihm verschwunden ist
(und) es schreit.

## English translation

You have sent him this:
You sent him grain
You sent him the disease
You sent him the illness of the eyes
They sent him the Kra [nkhe] it on the feet
They sent him the disease of the hands
You sent him the disease of the head
(so that) the heat (from) has disappeared
(and) it screams.
