## Translation

“He took back his anger,
 He took back his revenge,
 [F]ear he took back,
 He took back terror,
 He took back the disease of the heart,
 He took away his illness,
 He took away his old age.
 But he gave him back his youth,
 But he gave him back his fighting power."
