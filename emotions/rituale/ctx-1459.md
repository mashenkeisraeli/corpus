## Translation

Further, he says:
 “What is in these houses: the wrath of the weather god, fear, thunder, lightning,
 then the ḫuimma- shall let him go!
 But the beam of the floor below shall let him go !
 The warduli- of the hearth shall let him go!
 The threshold of the inner door shall let him go!"
