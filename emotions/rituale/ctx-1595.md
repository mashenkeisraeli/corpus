## Translation

And as soon as this all wool is clean (and) wiped,
 the gods also [… shall be pure from bloodshed, tears, perjury, cursing, all evil tongues, [and] before, now, and hereafter, from the controversy and from the court session of father, mother, brother, sister, [… , offspring, male servant, female servant, of the surrounding and interior countries.
 But now there is no transgression before these gods;
 [even in this last matter, they should be free from sin].
