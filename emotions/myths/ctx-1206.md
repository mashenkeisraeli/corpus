## Translation

We now encourage you with thick bread and libation.
 As a terrible one, become merciful!
 But [as an angry] man, say:
 "As if I had suffocated, but [I]m going to get better again!"
 They should be kind to you (and) appease you, the first-class [ ... ] (and) the waršula-!
 Turn to the king, the queen (and) the princes in goodness!
