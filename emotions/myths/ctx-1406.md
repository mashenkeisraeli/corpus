## Original Translation

Vor der Gottheit st[eht] ein -Baum.
Von dem -Baum aber (herab ist) das Vlies eines Schafes gehä[ngt],
[da]rin (ist) [das Gedeihen von] Getreide, Rind [(und) Schaf],
[da]rin (sind) lang[e] Jahre,
[und darin (ist) die s]anfte B[otschaft des Lamms].

## English Translation

A tree stood in front of the deity.
From the tree but (down) the fleece of a sheep is held,
[since] rin (is) [the thrive of] grain, beef [(and) sheep],
[since] rin (are) long [e] years,
[And in it () the s] begins b [Obordance of the lamb].
