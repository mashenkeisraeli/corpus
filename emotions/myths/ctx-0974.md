## Original Translation

„[Der Wettergott der] Ašmunikal zürnte!
[Er ...-e ] sein ... vo[rne ... ]
den [rechten] Schuh [aber zog er sich] link[s an].
Er drehte sich um
und g[ing] hinaus.
Dunst ergriff die Fen[ster],
Rau[ch ergriff] d[ie] Dachbalken (C: das Haus).
[Die Götter] erstick[ten] auf den [A]ltarpostamenten,
[ ... erstick]te,
im He[rd] erstickt[en die Holzscheite].“

## English Translation

"The weather god of Ašmunikal was angry! He ...-e his ... in front ... but he put the right shoe on the left. He turned around and went out. Mist took over the windows, smoke took over the roof beams (C: the house). The gods suffocated on the altar pedestals, ... suffocated, the logs in the hearth suffocated."
