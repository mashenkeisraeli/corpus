## Original translation

Der [Pförtner] öffnete [die sieben Türen,]
[die sieben Riegel zog er zurück.]
Unten [in] der dunklen [Erde stehen bronzene Kessel.]
[Ihre] Deck[el] (sind) aus Eisen,
ihr[e] Verschlüsse (sind) [aus Blei.]
Was [hineingeht],
ko[mmt] nicht [wieder daraus] hervor;
[es geht] dari[n zugrunde.]
[Auch des Wettergottes Wu]t, Zor[n], Fre[vel (und) Groll soll er einschließen,]
[und] es sollen nicht zurück[kommen]!

## English translation

The gatekeeper opened the seven doors and pulled back the seven bolts.Down in the dark earth there are bronze cauldrons.Their lids (are) of iron, their closures (are) of lead.What goes in does not come out again;it perishes in it.He shall also contain the wrath, wrath, iniquity (and) resentment of the weather god, and they shall not come back!
