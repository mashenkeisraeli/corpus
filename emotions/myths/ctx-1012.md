## Original Translation

[Wi]e [Malz] trocken (ist),
man es nicht auf das Feld [br]ingt
(und) es (nicht) zur Saat macht,
[e]s [nicht] zu Brot macht
(und) ins Vorratshaus [legt],
ebenso [soll] die Wut, der Zorn, [Frevel] (und) [Är]ger der Gottheit vertrocknen!

## English Translation

Just as malt (is dry), if one does not bring it into the field (and) make it into seed, or make it into bread (and) put it in the storehouse, so shall the anger, wrath, iniquity (and) anger of the deity dry up!
