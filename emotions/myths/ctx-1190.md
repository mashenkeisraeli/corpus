## Translation

[Elkun]irsa saw the weather god
 and asked him:
 "[Why] did you come here?" 
 The weather god (answered) as follows:
 "When I came to you and into your house,
 [ ... ] Ašertu sent me (her) daughters. 
 'Come, sleep with me!'
 [But I] said [no]. 
 But she got angry with me?
 and said [as follows]:
 'Be behind me!
 [Then] I'm behind you for you.
 With my [words] I press you down,
 but with my [spindle] I [prick] you.'
 That's why I came, my father.
 Nor did I come to you [through a messenger],
 I myself [came] to you. 
 Ashertu rejects you (in) your (capacity as) husband.
 [ ... ] your [wife],
 she moans at me:
 'Sleep [with me]!'"
 Elkunirša [began] to answer the weather god:
 "Go and ... [ ... ] her, [Ašertu], my [wife]! 
 Humiliate her!"

[ ... ] schickte mir Ašertu (ihre) Töchter.
‚Komm, schla[f] mit mir!‘

und sprach [folgender]maßen:
‚Sei hinter mir!
[Dann] bin ich für dich hinter dir.
Mit meinen [Worten] drücke ich dich [ni]eder,
mit meiner [] aber [stec]he ich dich.‘

sie jammert mich an:
‚[Schl]af [mit mir]!‘“

Demütige sie!“
