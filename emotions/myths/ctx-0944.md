## Original Translation

„Telipinu (ist) verärgert.
Seine Seele (und) [sein] Inne[res] erstickte(n) im Feuerholz.
Wie man dieses Feuer[holz] verbrannte,
ebenso soll[en] auch Telipinus Wu[t], Zorn, Frevel (und) Ärger verbrennen!
Wie [das Malz] trocken (ist),
[man] es nicht auf das Feld brin[gt]
und es (nicht) zur Saat macht,
es aber nicht zu Brot mac[ht]
(und) ins Vorratshaus legt,
ebenso sol[l] auch soll auch des Telipinu Wu[t, Zorn], Frevel (und) Ärger vertrocknen!

## English Translation

"Telipinu (is) angry. His soul (and) his insides were suffocated in the firewood. As this firewood was burned, so too shall Telipinu's anger, wrath, iniquity (and) anger be burned up! Just as the malt (is) dry, one does not bring it into the field and make it for seed, but does not make it into bread (and) put it in the storehouse, so also should Telipinu's anger, wrath, iniquity (and) angerdry up!
