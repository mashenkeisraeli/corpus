## Original Translation

„[Ich brannte hier] ü[ber] dem Wettergott [ganz weg]
[und brannte dort ganz weg].
[Ich nahm] dem Wettergott [sein Böses von seinem Körper],
ich n[ahm] seinen Frevel,
[ich nahm seine Wut],
[ich nahm seinen Zorn],
[ich] n[ahm] sein -,
[ich nahm (seinen) Groll].“

## English Translation

"I burned completely here over the weather god and burned completely away there. I took away the weather god's evil from his body, I took away his iniquity, I took away his anger, I took away his anger, I took away his -, I took away (his) resentment."
