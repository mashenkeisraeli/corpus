[ … das böse … ] nahm ich von seinem [K]örper.
se[ine] Wut [nahm ich, ]

## English Translation

And he... ...the evil... I took from his body.I took away his anger, I took away his wrath, I took away his iniquity, and also his anger.The evil tongue as well, the evil sole of the foot as well... the stiffness as well, the burden of spring as well, the burden of summer I took.
