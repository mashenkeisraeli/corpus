## Original Translation

„Wettergott!
Lass die Wut los!
[Lass] den Zor[n los!]
Las[s] den Groll los!
[Wi]e (das Wasser) des Rohres nicht rückwärts fließt,
ebens[o soll des Wettergottes Wut aber, Zo]rn (und) Groll [nicht] zurück[kommen]!“

## English Translation

"Weather God! Let go of the anger! Let go of the anger! Let go of the resentment! Just as (the water) of the pipe does not flow backwards, in the same way the weather god's anger, anger (and) resentment shall not come back!"
