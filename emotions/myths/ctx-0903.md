## Original Translation

[Auch der Ḫannaḫanna Wut, Zor]n,Frevel, [Ärger soll(en) nicht auf das gedeihende Feld,] in den [Wa]ld (und) den W[ein]garten [gehen]!

## English Translation

Even the Ḫannaḫanna anger, wrath, outrage, anger should not go into the flourishing field, into the forest (and) the vineyard!They should follow the path of the Sun Goddess of the Earth!
