## English translation

Pulli has just written me from Kašepura “As for the (plowed) fields of Tapikka and Taḫašara which were plowed, Ḫimmuili doesn’t give seed (for them). There is no seed." Those seeds, about which you, Ḫimmuili, spoke to me: “These are sown in Tapikka, These in Anziliya, These in Ḫariya, And these in Ḫanikkawa", - Where did those seeds go?
