## Translation

Because of my illness, my house has become a house of fear.
 Out of fear my soul drifts to another place.
 Like (one who is) sick for the period of the (whole) year,
 That is how I have become.
 But now illness and fear have become (too) much for me.
 My God, I tell YOU (now)

## Original Translation

Mein Haus ist für mich durch die Krankheit ein Haus der Angst geworden.
Vor Angst driftet mir meine Seele an einen anderen Ort.
Wie (einer, der) krank (ist) für den Zeitraum des (ganzen) Jahres,
ebenso bin auch ICH geworden.
Jetzt aber sind mir Krankheit und Angst (zu) viel geworden.
Mein Gott, ich sage es DIR (jetzt).

## English Translation

For me, my house has become a house of fear for me.
With fear, my soul drifts to another place.
How (one who) sick () for the period of the (whole) year,
I also became.
But now I have (too) a lot of illness and fear.
My god, I tell you (now).
