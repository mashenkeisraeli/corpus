## Translation

But in ME, Muwatalli, your servant, the soul will rejoice.
 I will exalt the weather god piḫaššašši, my lord.
 Both about the temples that I will build for you,
 as well as about the rites that I will perform for you,
 you will be happy, weather god piḫaššašši, my lord.
 The thick bread and the libation offering that I give to the weather god piḫaššašši, my lord
 Let me give that to you/him with happiness,
 But not with fear let me give.
 Weather god piḫaššašši, my lord, shine on me (from) above (as mildly) as the moonlight!
 Warm me from above like the sun god of heaven!

## Original Translation

In MIR aber, Muwatalli, deinem Diener, wird sich die Seele freuen.
Ich werde den Wettergott , meinen Herrn, erhöhen.
Sowohl über die Tempel, die ich dir errichten werde,
als auch über die Riten, die ich für dich [vollzie]hen werde,
wirst du dich, Wettergott , mein Herr, freuen.
Das Dickbrot und das Libationsopfer, das ich dem Wettergott , meinem Herrn, (jetzt) gebe,
das will ich ihm freudig geben!
Verängstigt aber will ich (es) dir nicht mehr geben!
Wettergott , mein Herr, scheine auf mich (von) oben (so mild) wie der Mondschein!
Wärme mich aber (von) oben wie der Sonnengott des Himmels!

## English Translation

But in me, Muwatalli, your servant, the soul will be happy.
I will increase the weather god, my gentleman.
Both about the temples that I will build on you,
as well as about the rites that I will be [fully] for you,
you will be happy, weather god, my Lord.
The thick bread and the Libation victim that I give the weather god, my gentleman, (now),
I want to give him that happily!
But I don't want to give you frightened!
Weather god, my master, seem on me (from) above (so mild) like the moonlight!
But warm me (from) above like the sun god of the sky!
