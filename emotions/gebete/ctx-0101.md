## Translation

When a [king] becomes god,
 whatever king comes to the power of
 and the (following) word does not speak to the countries:
 'I have made this contract to you.'"
 But when evil words were circulating among them against us,
 I was also afraid in that matter
 and I said as follows:
 "My father doesn't pay any attention to me at this time.
 Which people put pressure on me,
 in that matter my father does not look after me."
 In front of me (i.e. in my presence) no one disapproved of my father.
 If anyone says:
 "A terrible thing has been ordered.
 You shall not carry them out!"
 – Those who transmitted this word of my Father, 
 those same people also made that slander (against) me
 and [spoke]:
 "Why do they disapprove of your father before [you] (i.e. in your presence)?" -
 (then) I spoke (every time) as follows:
 "[If they] had made this as my slander before my father (i.e. in his presence),
 if (then) my father takes th[at ... ],
 will not evil happen to ME? "
