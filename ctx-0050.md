## Original Translation

[] be[ge]hren, [,]
[wel]che aber versuchen, [ ... ] Rhyt[a, Becher,] (Opfer)geräte aus [Silber (und) Gold] zu nehmen,
welche aber versuchen, euch Feld und eure Flur, [e]ure Weingärten, [] (und) eure Wälder zu verwüsten,
welche versuchen, sich eure Pflüger, Gärtner (und) Frauen des Mühlsteins zu nehmen,
jenen Feindesländern gib, Sonnengöttin von Arinna, meine Herrin, das böse Fieber, Seuche (und) Hunger!

## English Translation

[] be [ge] hren, [,]
[what] but try to take [...] rhyt [a, mug,] (victim) devices from [silver (and) gold],
But what try to devastate your fields and your hallway, [e] ure vineyards, [] (and)) your forests,
Which try to take your plows, gardeners (and) women of the millstone,
Those enemies, the sun goddess of Arinna, my mistress, the evil fever, disease (and) hunger!
